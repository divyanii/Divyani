FAQ Accordion

India is believed to have over 1,000 forts. Some sources estimate that there are over 4,000 forts and palaces across India.

Qila Mubarak in Bathinda, built around 90-110 AD, is one of India's oldest forts. It played a crucial role in history, including the imprisonment of Razia Sultan. The fort's ancient military architecture reflects the power struggles of past empires.

Determining the "most beautiful" fort is subjective, but some of the most renowned and visually stunning forts in India include Amber Fort (Jaipur), Mehrangarh Fort (Jodhpur), and the Red Fort (Delhi).
